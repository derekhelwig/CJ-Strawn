The Strawn Team

My husband, Steve, has been a Realtor since 1994 and I received my license in 1999. With the help of Steve's experience, I learned the ropes the first year and we have been "The Real Estate Dream Team" since then.

Buying or selling a home can be the largest purchase or sale in our clients' lives. Our goal is to make every transaction as smooth and stress free as possible. We are both involved in real estate at the local, state and national level to insure that we have the knoweledge, education and experience to do so.

When you choose to work with Steve and me, you can feel comfortable and confident that we will get the job done right!
